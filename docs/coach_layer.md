# Coach Layer

The Coach Layer is a future-facing, holistic layer that is intentionally decoupled from individual taxonomy slices. 

## Vision
The coach will consume insight packages from multiple taxonomies (Training, Nutrition, Labs, etc.) along with personal goals, constraints, and medical context to produce unified health recommendations.

## Core Characteristics
- **Holistic**: Considers the whole person, not just one lab test or one workout.
- **Adaptive**: Tunes itself based on the user's changing psychology and health goals.
- **Traceable**: Every recommendation must trace back through the insight packages to the source evidence.

## Future Scope
- **Conversational Interface**: Interaction with the coach via natural language.
- **Dynamic Benchmarking**: Moving comparison benchmarks based on the user's current profile segment (e.g., "athletic lifestyle" vs. "general health").
- **Recommendation Engine**: Generating specific, actionable advice across all health taxonomies.

Current taxonomy slices (like Thyroid) stop at generating metric_trend → alert → insight. They do NOT generate coach recommendations.
